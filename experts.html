James D. Downham		 Since 2006, Mr. Downham has been the President and CEO of Packaging Consortium (PAC), a not-for-profit organization. James' career was set in motion at Continental Can Company (CCC) in a management development program. He followed the sales and marketing career path and progressed through the ranks of Packaging Systems. This led to Vice Presidential roles of business units and ultimately to divisional President and CEO.
